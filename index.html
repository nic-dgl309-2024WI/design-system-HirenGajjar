Quick Answers

What types of plants and trees do you offer?

+

At Outback Nursery, we offer a diverse range of plants and trees to suit every garden and landscaping need. From vibrant flowering plants to majestic shade trees, we pride ourselves on providing a wide variety of species, including native and exotic options.

Do you provide delivery services?

+

Yes, we offer convenient delivery services to bring our plants and trees directly to your doorstep. Whether you're purchasing a single potted plant or a bulk order of trees for landscaping projects, our efficient delivery team ensures safe and timely delivery to your location.

Where is Outback Nursery located?

+

Outback Nursery is conveniently located at 6016 Headquarters Rd, Courtenay, BC V9J 1M7, making it easily accessible for customers in the Courtenay region and beyond.

What are your operating hours?

+

Our nursery is open 9 AM to 5 PM: Everyday (if weather permits). , ensuring that you have ample time to explore our selection of plants and trees at your convenience.

Do you offer planting and maintenance services?

+

Yes, in addition to offering a wide range of plants and trees, we provide comprehensive planting and maintenance services to help you bring your landscaping vision to life.
