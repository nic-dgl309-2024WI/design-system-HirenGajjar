Navigation Bar

Notation

A Navigation is an essential website feature that allows users to access information quickly and easily find what they need.
The navbar can also be considered as a site map of a website.

A navbar can consist of various components:
Logo, Navigaiton links, Dropdown menu, Seach bar, Call-to-actions, and Social media links.
